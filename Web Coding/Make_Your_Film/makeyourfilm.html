Here, upload four videos or images that you think represent Hong Kong the most, this can be food, scenery, activities, traffic... Anything that you relate with Hong Kong and you as a Hong Konger. When choosing your videos and images, landscape orientation will work better and try uploading high resolution videos to ensure clear quality of the film. If the uploaded materials are in portrait orientation, they will automatically be duplicated to fill the frame. Your uploaded materials will be randomly arranged to make the visual pattern. Once the collage is generated, the unique QR pattern will be created as well. You can download both of them and add that to your album collection, sharing them with your friends.
