PRINT YOUR T-SHIRT

What you wear can reflect your personality and identity. Printing your unique QR pattern on a merchandise will allow you to physically show your Hong Kong recognition.

You can choose from different styles and colors. There are three styles available - Phone Case, Plain T-shirt and T-shirt with Pocket. There is also a variety of colours you can choose from.
