We have been through rough times in recent years. At times we felt lost, questioning our identity and the definition of home. As tension increases in our community, we need to stay strong and rebuild our trust and connections with one another. Thus Home Kong is designed to reflect upon our identity as Hong Kongers, asking ourselves what makes our identity precious?

Here, you can showcase your emotions and memories of Hong Kong by creating your own personal film and a visual identity. With everyone’s effort and participation, we can build a diverse representation of Hong Kong, showing our love for this place and expressing how proud we are to be a Hong Konger.

Unique QR Pattern

Once you have created your film, you will receive a pattern collage which is extracted from your film.

This pattern is a one and only pattern that represent your unique interpretation of your identity as a Hong Konger.

You can share this as an image on your social media, showcasing your connection with Hong Kong.
